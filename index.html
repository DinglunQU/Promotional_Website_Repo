Biography

The Weeknd, Abel Makkonen Tesfaye, is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and record producer. He is known for his distinct voice and a blend of R&B, pop, and electronic music. Born on February 16, 1990, in Toronto, Canada, The Weeknd gained widespread recognition with his mixtapes before achieving mainstream success with his studio albums.
